Sales Enablement Manager: Role Formalization

A Strategic Business Case for 2026

Prepared for Chris Hickey, CBDO | Q1 2026 Strategy

Executive Summary: The Decision

The Opportunity

Nucleus Network’s global clinical trial expertise is a valuable asset, yet currently fragmented. This limits marketing impact, proposal quality, and revenue outcomes. With rising sponsor expectations for speed, organizations that convert expertise into fast, high-quality responses win more often.

The Strategy

Formalize a Global Revenue Enablement Platform acting as a closed-loop revenue engine:

  • Global expertise improves top-of-funnel quality.
  • AI accelerates RFIs and sponsor responses.
  • Proposal excellence increases conversion rates.

The Role

The Sales Enablement Manager functions as the Product Owner of this engine, owning:

  • Global expertise capture & reuse.
  • AI-enabled RFI & proposal systems.
  • Pipeline integrity & signal governance.

Business Impact

  • Higher-quality inbound sponsors.
  • Faster response times & shorter sales cycles.
  • Scale without incremental sales headcount.

Recommendation: Formalize role effective Q1 2026. This is a strategic investment in durable revenue capability, not headcount expansion.

The Role Already Exists

Current execution effectively bridges the gap between Marketing, Operations, and Business Development.

Pipeline Governance Active management keeps >70% deals active & reduces HREC violations.
Lead Infrastructure 100% of conference leads enriched & ingested into Salesforce.
Customer Signal End-to-end NPS program synthesis & reporting.
Event Logistics Managing global registrations, booths, and vendor logistics.

Business Impact: Speed & Risk

Speed to Lead

Reduces post-conference follow-up time from 5-7 days to <48 hours, significantly accelerating the initial sales cycle and deal velocity.

Forecast Reality

Strict governance ensures <25% HREC violations, removing "bloat" deals and ensuring Board-level reporting reflects revenue reality.

Risk Mitigation

Proactive management prevents compliance red flags and protects revenue integrity by owning the "clean data" engine.

Product Ownership: Global Revenue Enablement Platform

Purpose: Own and scale the systems that convert global expertise into revenue by improving funnel quality, speed, and conversion efficiency.

Foundational Layers

  • Global Revenue Signal Governance
    Own pipeline health, HREC integrity, and forecast credibility. Ensure board-level reporting reflects revenue reality.
  • Seller Productivity Platform
    Build and optimize dashboards that surface next-best actions. Drive adoption of tools that reduce admin burden.
  • Conference Revenue Engine
    Productize conference execution and lead capture. Ensure global events feed high-quality opportunities.

Growth & Scale Layers

  • Global Expertise Intelligence
    Convert institutional knowledge into structured insights. Feed marketing assets and sales narratives with real data.
  • AI-Enabled RFI & Proposal Systems
    Own rollout of AI agents for RFIs and proposals. Improve response speed, quality, and consistency at scale.
  • BD Playbooks & Operating Standards
    Standardize best practices for CRO and Direct engagement. Increase repeatable execution across regions.

This role functions as the Product Owner of Nucleus’s revenue enablement engine, accountable for outcomes — not tasks.
